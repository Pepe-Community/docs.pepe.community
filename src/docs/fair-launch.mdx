The launching time will be announced 1 week in advance on Twitter and Telegram channel.

We will publish the listing date on PancakeSwap. There is no chance for the team or anyone can buy prior to the community.

On the launching date:

- **The first 1 hour**: the max transaction is limit is 0.01% supply (100 000 000 000 $PEPE) per transaction
- **After 1 hour of launching**, the transaction limit is set to 0.05% (0.01% for selling) as described at [no whale feature](/no-whale)
